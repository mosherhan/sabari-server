About Us

Sabarigiri Industries is one of the leading companies specializing in laser cutting and steel ornamentation, located in Kaduvinal, Vallikunnam, Alappuzha, Kerala.

We undertake a wide range of projects involving Metal Sheet (MS), Stainless Steel, Brass, and Titanium. Our expertise extends to precision laser cutting, making us the first in Kerala to offer specialized laser cutting services for all types of pipes.

With a commitment to quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction, we strive to deliver high-precision metal fabrication solutions tailored to diverse industrial and commercial needs.
