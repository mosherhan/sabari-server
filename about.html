Who We Are ?

Sabarigiri Industries is the one of the most leading Laser cutting and steel ornamentation company located in Kaduvinal, Vallikunnam Alappuzha, Kerala, We are undertaking all kinds of steel MS and CS, Stainless Steel and First in Kerala for all kinds of pipes LASER CUTTING

Why Us

We specialize in laser cutting for MS, CS, Stainless Steel ensuring precision and quality.

As Kerala’s first to offer laser cutting for all types of pipes, we provide innovative solutions for structural and industrial needs.

Our advanced technology delivers accuracy, clean finishes, and minimal wastage, ideal for custom and large-scale projects.

Customer satisfaction is our priority, offering reliable, high-quality cutting solutions tailored to your requirements.

We Do

  • Laser cutting of 0.1mm to 15mm thick CS and MS Sheets and Plates
  • Laser cutting of 0.1mm to 10mm Stainless Steel sheets and plates
  • Laser cutting of 0.1 mm to 10 mm Aluminum sheets and plates

  • Laser cutting of metal sheets
  • First in Kerala ¼” to 4” Stainless steel, Aluminum, Tubes and Pipes Laser Cutting
